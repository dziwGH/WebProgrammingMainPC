David Martinez

Fun fact: David does not like carbonated drinks!

David Martinez is the main character in the animated series "Cyberpunk: Edgerunners". This 10 episode, action packed, romance sub-themed series is worth the watch. It teaches us about the importance of our sanity and how fragile human life truly is. David always chases his dreams to his fullest potential no matter the risk
